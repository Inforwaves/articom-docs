## Introduction

This guide will help you set up your Articom account and get started with creating your first AI Assistant.  
If you already have an account, skip to [**Create Your First Assistant**](/features/chatbot).

---

## 1. Create an Articom Account

To begin, navigate to the Articom dashboard:

**https://dashboard.articom.io**

You will see the **Sign In** and **Sign Up** options.

### Sign Up

If you don't have an account, click **Sign In**.  
You can sign up using:

- **Google**
- **GitHub**
- **Email and Password**

![Sign Up Interface](/images/features/get-started/signup-google.png)

### Sign Up with Email

Fill in the required fields:

- **First Name**
- **Last Name**
- **Email**
- **Password**

![Email Sign Up Form](/images/features/get-started/signup-gmail.png)

Check the box to agree to the Terms and Privacy Policy, then click **Sign Up**.

### Email Verification  
You will receive a verification email.  
Click **Verify Email** to activate your account.

---

## 2. Complete Onboarding

After verifying your email, you will be guided through a simple onboarding flow.  
This helps us customize your workspace.

### You will be asked:

#### 1. **Phone Number**
Used for account recovery and basic security.

![Phone Number Input](/images/features/get-started/phone.png)

#### 2. **How did you hear about Articom?**
Choose an option such as:
- Social Media  
- Google Search  
- Referral  
- Event / Workshop  

![User Source Screen](/images/features/get-started/source.png)

#### 3. **Your Role**
Select what best describes you:
- Student  
- Employee  
- Self-employed  
- Founder  

If you choose **Employee**, you will be asked:

- Company Name  
- Industry  

#### 4. **Your Work Function**
Choose your field:
- Product Management  
- Sales  
- Marketing  
- Engineering  
- Customer Support  
- Operations  

![Role Selection Screen](/images/features/get-started/usecases.png)

After completing these steps, click **Continue**.

---

## 3. Explore Your Dashboard

Once onboarding is done, you will be redirected to the Articom dashboard.

You will have access to:

- **AI Assistant**
- **Knowledge Base**
- **Analytics**
- **Integrations**
- **Manage Assistants**

![Dashboard Overview](/images/features/assistant/dashboard.png)

Use the left navigation menu to move between features.

---

## 4. Create Your First AI Assistant

To start building your assistant:

1. Go to **AI Assistant** from the sidebar.  
2. Click **Create New Assistant**.

You will see three options:

- **Create from Scratch**
- **Easy Create & Customize**
- **Build with AI**

![Create Assistant Options](/images/features/assistant/own-assistants.png)

➡ For the complete step-by-step guide, see:  
**[AI Assistant](/features/chatbot)**

---

## 5. Connect Your First Channel (Optional)

Once your assistant is ready, you can deploy it by connecting:

- WhatsApp  
- Instagram  
- Facebook Messenger  
- Website Widget  
- Voice Agent  

Go to **Integrations** → choose a channel → follow the setup steps.

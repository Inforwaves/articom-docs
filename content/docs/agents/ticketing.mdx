## Overview

The Ticketing Agent automates support ticket creation, routing, and resolution, ensuring customer issues are handled efficiently and effectively.

## Features

### Ticket Management
- Create and categorize tickets
- Route to appropriate teams
- Track ticket status
- Prioritize based on urgency
- Manage SLA compliance

### Issue Resolution
- Provide instant solutions for common problems
- Guide users through troubleshooting
- Access knowledge base articles
- Escalate complex issues
- Follow up on resolutions

### Analytics & Reporting
- Track resolution times
- Monitor ticket volume
- Identify common issues
- Generate performance reports

## Best Practices

1. **Quick Triage**: Rapidly categorize and prioritize tickets
2. **Clear Communication**: Keep customers informed of progress
3. **Knowledge Base**: Maintain updated solutions database
4. **Automation**: Resolve common issues automatically

## Use Cases

- IT support tickets
- Customer service requests
- Bug reports
- Feature requests
- Account issues
- Technical problems

# Agents Overview

AI agents are intelligent systems designed to perform specific tasks autonomously. They can understand context, make decisions, and interact with users in natural language.

## What are Agents?

Agents are specialized AI assistants that can:

- Understand and process natural language
- Execute specific business functions
- Learn from interactions
- Provide contextual responses
- Integrate with existing systems

## Use Cases

Agents can be deployed across various scenarios including:

- Customer support and service
- E-commerce assistance
- Event management
- Restaurant reservations
- Ticketing systems
- Live chat support
- Complaint handling

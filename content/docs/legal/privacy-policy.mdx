# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 20, 2025**

## Introduction

Articom ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered customer engagement platform.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Name and contact details
- Email address and phone number
- Company information
- Billing information and payment details

**Content You Submit**
- Conversation data and chat transcripts
- Training data for AI models
- Custom configurations and settings
- Feedback and support requests

### Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data**
- Log data and analytics
- Device information and IP addresses
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent
- Interaction patterns

**Cookies and Tracking**
- Session cookies
- Performance cookies
- Analytics cookies
- Preference cookies

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

### Service Delivery
- Provide and maintain our services
- Process transactions and send notifications
- Respond to your requests and inquiries
- Deliver customer support

### Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Develop new features and functionality
- Improve AI model accuracy
- Enhance user experience

### Communication
- Send service-related announcements
- Provide updates and newsletters
- Conduct surveys and research
- Share marketing communications (with consent)

### Security and Compliance
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Ensure platform security
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our terms of service

## How We Share Your Information

### Service Providers
We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:
- Cloud hosting providers
- Payment processors
- Analytics services
- Customer support tools

### Business Transfers
Information may be transferred in connection with:
- Merger or acquisition
- Sale of assets
- Bankruptcy proceedings
- Corporate restructuring

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law:
- Court orders and subpoenas
- Government investigations
- Protection of rights and safety
- Prevention of fraud or illegal activity

### With Your Consent
We may share information with your explicit consent for:
- Integration with third-party services
- Participation in joint programs
- Other purposes you approve

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

**Technical Safeguards**
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Regular security audits
- Penetration testing

**Organizational Measures**
- Access controls and authentication
- Employee training and policies
- Incident response procedures
- Data backup and recovery

### Data Retention

We retain your information:
- Account data: Duration of account plus 90 days
- Conversation data: As specified in your plan
- Analytics data: 24 months
- Backup data: 30 days

You can request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

**Right to Access**
- Request a copy of your data
- View collected information
- Export your data

**Right to Rectification**
- Correct inaccurate information
- Update your profile
- Modify settings and preferences

**Right to Deletion**
- Delete your account
- Request data removal
- Opt-out of data collection

**Right to Portability**
- Download your data
- Transfer to another service
- Receive in standard format

### Communication Preferences

**Email Communications**
- Opt-out of marketing emails
- Manage notification preferences
- Unsubscribe from newsletters

**Cookie Settings**
- Manage cookie preferences
- Disable non-essential cookies
- Control tracking settings

## Regional Privacy Rights

### European Union (GDPR)

EU residents have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to object to processing
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority
- Right to withdraw consent

Data Protection Officer: privacy@articom.io

### California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have rights under CCPA:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or shared
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

**Do Not Sell My Personal Information**

We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@articom.io

### Other Jurisdictions

We comply with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where we operate.

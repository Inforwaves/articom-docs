# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Effective Date: November 20, 2025**

## Overview

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") describes the uptime and support commitments that Articom provides to customers using our AI-powered customer engagement platform.

## Definitions

**"Service"** means the Articom platform and associated services as described in your subscription agreement.

**"Uptime"** means the percentage of time the Service is available and operational during a calendar month.

**"Downtime"** means any period when the Service is unavailable or not functioning as intended.

**"Maintenance Window"** means scheduled maintenance periods announced at least 48 hours in advance.

**"Service Credits"** means credits applied to your account as compensation for SLA breaches.

## Service Availability

### Uptime Commitment

| Plan | Monthly Uptime Guarantee | Maximum Downtime/Month |
|------|--------------------------|------------------------|
| **Enterprise** | 99.99% | ~4 minutes |
| **Professional** | 99.9% | ~43 minutes |
| **Standard** | 99.5% | ~3.6 hours |
| **Starter** | 99.0% | ~7.2 hours |

### Uptime Calculation

```
Uptime % = (Total Minutes - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes × 100
```

**Excluded from Downtime:**
- Scheduled maintenance windows
- Issues caused by factors outside our control
- Force majeure events
- Customer's internet or network issues
- Third-party service failures
- Customer misuse or abuse

**Low (P4)**

- General questions

**Duration:** Maximum 4 hours

## Service Credits

### Credit Calculation

If we fail to meet our uptime commitment, you are eligible for service credits:

| Uptime Achieved | Service Credit |
|-----------------|----------------|
| < 99.99% to ≥ 99.9% | 10% of monthly fee |
| < 99.9% to ≥ 99.0% | 25% of monthly fee |
| < 99.0% | 50% of monthly fee |

### Claiming Credits

To receive service credits:

1. **Submit Request:** Within 30 days of the incident
2. **Provide Details:** Include dates, times, and impact description
3. **Verification:** We will verify the claim within 10 business days
4. **Application:** Credits applied to next billing cycle

**Maximum Credit:** 50% of monthly subscription fee

### Credit Limitations

Service credits are:
- Your sole remedy for SLA breaches
- Not refundable for cash
- Valid for 12 months from issuance
- Applied automatically to future invoices

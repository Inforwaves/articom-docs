## Overview

After creating your Assitant, you can view, enable services for your assistant. 

## Get started

Navigate to **AI Assistant** section and under **Your AI Assistants**, you can see the newly created assistant. Click on it to manage and view services available for your Assistant.

![View your Assistants](/images/features/assistant/own-assistants.png)

## Featured Activities 

You can see the Activities and Services available for assistant. It includes:
* **Dashboard** - Get analytics for your own assistant
* **Manage Services** - View and Manage Services for your Assistant
* **Knowladge Base** - Create, Connect customized knowledge base
* **Agents** 
* **Workflows** 
* **Deployment** 

Choose this option if you want **full control** and want to configure the assistant manually.

### Dashboard 

View Dashboard of your Assistant for Real-Time analytics of **Your AI Assistants, Knowladge bases, Total Cost** and **Token Usage (Daily/ Weekly/ Monthly)**. 
![Features for Assistant](/images/features/assistant/dashboard.png)

### Services 

Find the services available and connect them to your Assistant. 

* Chatbot Service

### 1. Chatbot Service

First **Enable** the **Chatbot Service** and once enabled, you can see the Chatbot **Active** status at the top. 

![Allow Chatbot service](/images/features/assistant/allow-chatbot-service.png)


Now you can **Customize Your Chatbot** here. First configure the chatbot services that you want to include.

![Features for Assistant](/images/features/assistant/configure-chatbot-service.png)

#### Next Step: Customize Your Chabot per Your preferences

This section allows you to update then UI of your chatbot with your logo, colour theme etc. In the **Basic Settings** you can customize the UI for both **Light-Dark Theme** based on your preferences. It includes a **Live-Preview** where you can view changes in **different screen sizes (Mobile and Desktop View)**. 
![Customize Chatbot UI](/images/features/assistant/customize-chatbot.png)

## Overview

Articom platform provides you a flexible and intuitive environment for build, deploy, and manage intelligent assistants that automate conversations, support tasks, and deliver information 24/7. 

You can build assistants instantly, using AI to guide you, or through fully customizable configurations.

## Get started

When creating a new assistant, you will be presented with three options:

* **Create from Scratch** 
* **Easy Create & Customize** (Template-based) 
* **Build with AI** 

Each method suits different levels of experience and speed of deployment.

### 3.1. Method 1: Creating a New Assistant from Scratch

Choose this option if you want **full control** and want to configure the assistant manually.

![Create Assistant Interface](/images/features/assistant/chatbot-createNew.png)

#### How it works

1.  Click **Create from Scratch**.
2.  Provide basic details:
    * **Assistant Name** 
    * **Role** (e.g., Support Agent, Sales Bot, Lead Assistant)
3.  Select **Languages**.
4.  Select the **AI modal** for your chatbot.
5.  Define the assistant behavior in the description:
    * **Tone and personality** 
    * **Response style** (detailed, concise, friendly, formal) 
6.  Attach data sources:
    * Previously created **Knowledge Bases** 
    * **Documents** 
7.  Save and proceed to testing.

### 3.2. Method 2: Easy Create & Customize (Template-Based)

This option provides **ready-made templates** for common business needs, perfect for users who want a working assistant quickly. Each pre-built template comes with industry-specific knowledge and workflows that you can customize.

![Template Selection Interface](/images/features/assistant/chatbot-templates.png)

### 3.3. Method 3: Build with AI

This mode allows you to create a complete assistant with help of AI. You can create fully customized AI assistant according to your preferences.

![Build Assistant with AI Interface](/images/features/assistant/chatbot-withAI.png)


After successful creation, you can see **your AI Assistant** visible in **Your Assistant Section**. Click on it to manage and view services available for your Assistant.

![View your Assistants](/images/features/assistant/own-assistants.png)

## Use Cases of AI Assistant

### Customer Support
Handle common inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and escalate complex problems.

### Lead Qualification
Engage visitors, collect information, and qualify leads for sales teams.

### E-commerce
Assist with product discovery, answer questions, and guide purchases.

### Appointment Booking
Schedule meetings, appointments, and reservations automatically.

### FAQ Automation
Provide instant answers to frequently asked questions.

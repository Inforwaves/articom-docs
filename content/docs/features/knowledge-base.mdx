## Overview

The Knowledge Base is a centralized content management system that stores information, FAQs, documentation, and training data used by your AI agents to answer customer questions.

## Key Features

### Rich Content Editor 

Create and format content in your knowledgeBase with ease

![Edit knowledgeBase](/images/features/knowladebase/edit-knowlagebase.png)

### Multi-format Support 

Supports multiple formats including **PDF, TXT, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX**, and **Website URLs**.

![multipe content formats](/images/features/knowladebase/multi-format-content.png)

## Overview

Comprehensive analytics and reporting tools to monitor agent performance, understand user behavior, and optimize customer interactions.

## Get Started 

The Analytics Dashboard is the centralized hub for tracking and optimizing your AI performance. Tracks performance metrics, user behavior, and conversation analytics to optimize your agents.

### Real-time Analytics and Insights from Main Dashboard

Monitor and get insights from real-time analytics from our **Main Dashboard** which includes analytics for all the AI Assistants, Agents created via Articom Platform

![Dashboard of Assistant](/images/features/analytics/dashboard.png)



### Real-time Analytics for your AI Assistants

Visit **Dashboard** section in **your AI Assistant** to monitor **only your Assistant performance** and the total price, token usage based on **Usage** (Daily/ Weekly/ Monthly).

![Dashboard of Assistant](/images/features/assistant/dashboard.png)




## Use Cases

* **Customer Service Optimization:** Identify bottlenecks and improve support efficiency.

* **Product Insights:** Understand customer needs and feature requests.

* **Business Intelligence:** Track business impact of AI agents.

* **Quality Assurance:** Monitor conversation quality and agent accuracy.

* **Capacity Planning:** Forecast demand and optimize resources.

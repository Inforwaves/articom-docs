## Chat Capabilities

### Information Retrieval
```plaintext
Customer: "What are your business hours?"
Agent: "We're open Monday-Friday 9 AM to 6 PM, and Saturday 10 AM to 4 PM. 
        We're closed on Sundays. How can I help you today?"

Customer: "Do you offer free shipping?"
Agent: "Yes! We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. 
        Orders typically arrive within 5-7 business days. 
        Would you like expedited shipping for faster delivery?"
```

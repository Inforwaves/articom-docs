## Customer Support Scenarios

### Product Inquiries
```plaintext
Customer: "Do you have this in size medium?"
Agent: "Let me check availability for you. Yes, the Classic Blue T-Shirt is available 
        in Medium! Would you like me to add it to your cart?"

Customer: "What's it made of?"
Agent: "It's 100% organic cotton, pre-shrunk and machine washable. It's one of our 
        best sellers! Very comfortable and breathable."
```

### Order Tracking
```plaintext
Customer: "Where's my order?"
Agent: "I can help you track your order! May I have your order number?"

Customer: "#12345"
Agent: "Order #12345 was shipped on Nov 18 and is currently in transit. 
        Expected delivery: Nov 21. Track it here: [link]. Anything else?"
```

### Returns Processing
```plaintext
Customer: "I need to return an item"
Agent: "I'm sorry it didn't work out! I can help with that. 
        What's your order number?"

Customer: "#54321"
Agent: "I've initiated a return for order #54321. 
        You'll receive a prepaid return label via email within 5 minutes. 
        Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item."
```

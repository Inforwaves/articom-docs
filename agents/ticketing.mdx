## Booking Experience

```plaintext
User: "I want to book movie tickets"
Agent: "Great! Which movie would you like to watch?"

User: "The new Marvel movie"
Agent: "Avengers: The New Era is playing at 3 locations near you. 
        Which date and time works best?"

User: "Tomorrow at 7 PM"
Agent: "Perfect! Tomorrow at 7:00 PM. How many tickets?"

User: "2 tickets"
Agent: "I'll show you available seats. [Displays seat map] 
        Which seats would you like?"

User: "G7 and G8"
Agent: "Excellent choice! 2 tickets for Avengers at 7:00 PM tomorrow, seats G7-G8. 
        Total: $28. Ready to confirm?"
```

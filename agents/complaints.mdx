## Complaint Handling Process

```plaintext
Customer: "My order arrived damaged"
Agent: "I'm very sorry to hear that your order arrived damaged. I understand how 
        frustrating this must be. Let me help you resolve this right away. 
        May I have your order number?"

Customer: "#12345"
Agent: "Thank you. I've pulled up order #12345. I can offer you:
        1. Full refund
        2. Replacement shipped with express delivery
        Which would you prefer?"

Customer: "Replacement please"
Agent: "I've arranged for a replacement to be shipped via express delivery today. 
        You'll receive it by Nov 21. I've also applied a 20% discount to your next 
        order as an apology. Is there anything else I can help with?"
```

## Features

### Intelligent Triage
- Categorize complaints automatically
- Assess severity and urgency
- Route to appropriate department
- Prioritize critical issues

### Resolution Options
- Refunds and replacements
- Compensation offers
- Service credits
- Escalation to management

### Follow-Up System
- Track resolution progress
- Automated status updates
- Satisfaction surveys
- Feedback collection

## Benefits

- **Faster Resolution**: Instant acknowledgment and action
- **Consistent Process**: Every complaint handled properly
- **Customer Retention**: Turn complainers into loyal customers
- **Insights**: Identify systemic issues from complaint patterns

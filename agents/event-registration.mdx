## What It Can Do

### Event Inquiry Handling
```plaintext
User: "What time does the conference start?"
Agent: "The Annual Tech Conference 2025 starts at 9:00 AM on December 5th. 
        Doors open at 8:30 AM for registration. Would you like to register?"

User: "Yes, how do I register?"
Agent: "Great! I can help you register right now. May I have your full name please?"
```

### Registration Processing
- Collect attendee information
- Validate registration details
- Process payment (if applicable)
- Send confirmation emails
- Issue digital tickets

### Event Updates
- Send schedule changes
- Notify about speaker updates
- Share venue information
- Provide parking details

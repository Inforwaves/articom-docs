## Booking Conversation Example

```plaintext
Customer: "I'd like to make a reservation for dinner"
Agent: "I'd be happy to help you make a reservation! 
        For what date would you like to book?"

Customer: "This Saturday at 7 PM"
Agent: "Let me check our availability for Saturday at 7:00 PM. 
        How many guests will be joining you?"

Customer: "Party of 4"
Agent: "Perfect! I have a table for 4 available at 7:00 PM this Saturday. 
        May I have your name and phone number?"

Customer: "John Smith, 555-1234"
Agent: "Excellent! Your reservation is confirmed for Saturday at 7:00 PM for 4 guests. 
        You'll receive a confirmation text shortly. Any special requests?"

Customer: "Window seat if possible"
Agent: "I've noted your preference for a window seat. We'll do our best to accommodate! 
        Looking forward to seeing you Saturday!"
```

## Features

### Real-Time Availability
- Check table availability instantly
- Prevent overbooking
- Optimize seating arrangements
- Handle peak hours efficiently

### Modification & Cancellation
- Allow customers to change reservations
- Process cancellations automatically
- Update waitlist when tables free up
- Rebook cancelled slots

### Customer Preferences
- Remember repeat customers
- Save dietary restrictions
- Track seating preferences
- Special occasion notes

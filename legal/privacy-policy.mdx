## Privacy Policy

At Articom, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

## Information We Collect

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Customer Data">
    Information you provide to train and operate your AI agents:
    - Business information and FAQs
    - Product documentation
    - Customer service scripts
    - Knowledge base content
    
    **You retain full ownership of your Customer Data.**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="End User Data">
    Information from conversations with your AI agents:
    - Messages and conversation history
    - Contact information (name, email, phone)
    - Interaction metadata (timestamps, channels)
    - Voice recordings (for voice agents)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Account Information">
    Information for your Articom account:
    - Company name and contact details
    - Billing information
    - User accounts and permissions
    - Usage and analytics data
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Technical Data">
    Automatically collected information:
    - IP addresses and device information
    - Browser type and version
    - Usage patterns and performance data
    - Cookies and tracking technologies
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Services**: Operate and maintain the Articom Platform
2. **Improve AI**: Train and improve our AI models (with your permission)
3. **Customer Support**: Respond to inquiries and provide assistance
4. **Analytics**: Generate insights and performance metrics
5. **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud and security threats
6. **Communication**: Send service updates and important notices
7. **Compliance**: Meet legal and regulatory requirements

## Data Processing & Third-Party AI

</Warning>

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- **Access**: Request copies of your data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Portability**: Export your data in standard formats
- **Objection**: Object to certain data processing
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data

### How to Exercise Rights
Contact us at: privacy@articom.io

## Data Retention

- **Customer Data**: Retained while account is active
- **Conversation History**: Configurable retention periods
- **Deleted Data**: Permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Backups**: Removed from backups within 90 days

## GDPR Compliance

For European users:

- **Lawful Basis**: Contract performance and legitimate interests
- **Data Controller**: You are the controller, we are the processor
- **DPA Available**: Data Processing Agreement upon request
- **EU Data Centers**: Available for Enterprise customers
- **Subject Rights**: Full GDPR rights support

## Children's Privacy

Articom is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

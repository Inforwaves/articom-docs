    - **Feedback**: Customer feedback grants Articom perpetual license to use
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="7. Confidentiality">
    Both parties must:
    - Protect Confidential Information with reasonable care
    - Use information solely for Agreement purposes
    - Not disclose except as required by law
    
    Excludes publicly known information and independently developed data.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="8. Data Privacy & Security">
    - Articom implements appropriate security measures
    - Processes Customer Data per lawful instructions
    - Privacy Policy governs personal data use
    - Customer responsible for End User awareness
  </Accordion>

    - Either party may terminate for material breach (30 days notice)
    - Immediate termination for bankruptcy/insolvency
    - Customer data exportable within 30 days post-termination

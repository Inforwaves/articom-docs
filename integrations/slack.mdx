## Use Cases

### IT Helpdesk
```plaintext
Employee: /articom reset password
Bot: I can help you reset your password. For security, please visit:
     https://portal.company.com/reset
     You'll receive a reset link via email in 2-3 minutes.
```

### HR Support
```plaintext
Employee: What's the PTO policy?
Bot: Employees receive 15 days of PTO annually, plus 10 holidays.
     PTO accrues monthly and can be used after 90 days.
     Check your balance at: https://hr.company.com/pto
```

### Onboarding
- New hire welcome messages
- Equipment request automation
- Document access
- Training schedules

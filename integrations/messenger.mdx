## Overview

Connect your Facebook Page to Articom and provide automated customer support through Messenger. Handle page messages, provide instant responses, and integrate with Facebook Business Suite.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Facebook Page">
    Ensure you have admin access to your Facebook Page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect">
    In Articom: Integrations → Messenger → Connect with Facebook
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Grant necessary permissions to Articom
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set up automated responses and greetings
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Features

- Instant replies
- Persistent menu
- Get started button
- Quick replies
- Generic templates
- Button templates
- Receipt templates

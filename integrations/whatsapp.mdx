## Overview

Integrate Articom with WhatsApp Business API to provide automated customer support through the world's most popular messaging app. Handle inquiries, send notifications, and engage customers where they already are.

## Setup Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="WhatsApp Business Account">
    Create a WhatsApp Business account if you don't have one
  </Step>
  <Step title="Facebook Business Manager">
    Set up Facebook Business Manager and verify your business
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Articom">
    In Articom dashboard, go to Integrations → WhatsApp → Connect
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set up welcome messages and automated responses
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test">
    Send a test message to verify the integration
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Capabilities

### Message Types
- Text messages with formatting
- Images and videos
- Documents (PDF, DOC, etc.)
- Location sharing
- Contact cards
- Interactive buttons
- List messages

### Automation Rules
```plaintext
Trigger: Customer sends "Hi"
Action: Send welcome message with menu options

Trigger: Customer asks about hours
Action: Send business hours and location

Trigger: Keyword "order status"
Action: Request order number and provide tracking
```

## Best Practices

<Warning>
  WhatsApp has strict policies against spam. Only send messages to customers who have opted in.
</Warning>

- Respond within 24 hours to maintain quality
- Use templates for notifications
- Keep messages conversational
- Respect WhatsApp's commerce policies

## Sample Voice Interactions

### Example 1: Appointment Booking

```plaintext
Voice Agent: "Thank you for calling ABC Clinic. How may I help you today?"

Caller: "I'd like to book an appointment with Dr. Smith"

Voice Agent: "I'd be happy to help you schedule an appointment with Dr. Smith. 
             What day works best for you?"

Caller: "Next Tuesday afternoon"

Voice Agent: "Let me check Dr. Smith's availability for Tuesday afternoon. 
             I have 2:00 PM or 4:30 PM available. Which time would you prefer?"

Caller: "2:00 PM works"

Voice Agent: "Perfect! I've booked you for Tuesday at 2:00 PM with Dr. Smith. 
             You'll receive a confirmation text shortly. Is there anything else?"

Caller: "No, that's all"

Voice Agent: "Great! We'll see you Tuesday at 2:00 PM. Have a wonderful day!"
```

### Example 2: Order Tracking

```plaintext
Voice Agent: "Hello! Thank you for calling XYZ Store. How can I assist you?"

Caller: "I want to track my order"

Voice Agent: "I can help with that! May I have your order number please?"

Caller: "It's 12345"

Voice Agent: "Thank you. Let me check order 12345 for you. 
             Your order was shipped on November 18th and is currently in transit. 
             The expected delivery date is November 21st. 
             Would you like me to text you the tracking link?"

Caller: "Yes please"

Voice Agent: "I've sent the tracking link to your registered mobile number. 
             Is there anything else I can help you with today?"
```

## Best Practices

<Note>
  **Pro Tip**: Always offer the option to speak with a human agent, especially for frustrated or confused callers.
</Note>

1. **Keep It Natural**: Use conversational language, not robotic responses
2. **Be Clear**: Speak slowly and clearly, especially when providing important information
3. **Confirm Understanding**: Repeat back important details (dates, times, names)
4. **Set Expectations**: Let callers know when they'll receive confirmations or callbacks
5. **Easy Escalation**: Make it simple to reach a human agent when needed

## Overview

Articom's Dynamic Knowledge Base is the brain of your AI agents. Keep your chatbot and voice agents accurate with an easy-to-update knowledge base that reduces workload and enhances customer experience.

## Creating Your Knowledge Base

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather Content">
    Collect all relevant documents, FAQs, and information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize by Topics">
    Group related information into categories
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload to Articom">
    Import documents or enter information directly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Test">
    Test AI responses to ensure accuracy
  </Step>
  <Step title="Maintain & Update">
    Regularly update with new information
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Note>
  **Tip**: Keep your knowledge base current by reviewing and updating it monthly.
</Note>

- Use clear, concise language
- Keep information up-to-date
- Include specific examples
- Cover edge cases
- Regular quality checks

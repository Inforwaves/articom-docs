## Overview

Articom's Analytics Dashboard provides comprehensive insights into your customer support operations. Monitor chatbot performance, track engagement metrics, and uncover growth opportunities with intuitive visualizations and real-time data.

## Dashboard Features

### Real-Time Monitoring
- **Live Conversations**: See active chats and calls in real-time
- **Agent Status**: Monitor AI and human agent availability
- **Queue Management**: Track waiting customers and response times
- **Alert System**: Get notified of issues requiring attention

### Performance Analytics

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="ChatBot Performance">
    - **Automation Rate**: Percentage of queries handled automatically
    - **Confidence Scores**: AI's certainty in responses
    - **Common Queries**: Most frequently asked questions
    - **Knowledge Gaps**: Questions the bot struggled with
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Voice Agent Metrics">
    - **Call Volume**: Total calls handled by voice agents
    - **Average Call Duration**: Time spent on each call
    - **Transfer Rate**: Calls escalated to human agents
    - **Language Distribution**: Languages used by callers
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Channel Performance">
    - **WhatsApp**: Message volume and response times
    - **Messenger**: Engagement and resolution rates
    - **Instagram**: DM handling and customer satisfaction
    - **Voice**: Call metrics and completion rates
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Best Practices

<Note>
  **Pro Tip**: Review your analytics dashboard weekly to identify trends and optimization opportunities.
</Note>

1. **Set Benchmarks**: Establish baseline metrics for comparison
2. **Monitor Trends**: Look for patterns over time, not just snapshots
3. **Act on Insights**: Use data to improve knowledge base and workflows
4. **Share Reports**: Keep stakeholders informed with scheduled reports
5. **Track ROI**: Measure cost savings and business impact
